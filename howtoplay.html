HOW TO PLAY

You are an up-and-coming bass player and you’ve found a Battle of the Basses! In this game, you’ll compete against another player (the computer) to see who can do a better job playing the cover song.

When you see the green circle close around each note, hit the button that plays that note on your keyboard (see Controls). If you reach a combo of 40 notes in a row, you will receive STAR POWER for a x2 multiplier. Do your best!
